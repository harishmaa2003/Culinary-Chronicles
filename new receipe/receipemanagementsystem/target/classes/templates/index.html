Mushroom Kadai

To prepare Mushroom Kadai, start by dry roasting and grinding cumin seeds, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, dried red chilies, and black peppercorns into a coarse powder. Heat oil in a kadai, add cumin seeds, onion, and ginger-garlic paste, sauté until golden. Add turmeric, red chili, and coriander powders, cook with tomato until softened. Add mushrooms and bell pepper, cook until mushrooms are tender. Stir in the kadai masala, garam masala, and salt, cook briefly. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti or rice. Enjoy your flavorful Mushroom Kadai!

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients: Cake: 1¾ cups flour 1½ tsp baking powder 1½ tsp baking 2 cups sugar 1 tsp salt 2 large eggs 1 cup milk 1 cup coco powder ½ cup vegetable oil 2 tsp vanilla extract 1 cup boiling water Frosting: 1 cup butter, softened 3½ cups powdered sugar ½ cup milk 2 tsp vanilla extract Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans. Combine dry cake ingredients. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat for 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water. Pour into pans and bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool. For frosting, beat butter, then add remaining ingredients. Frost cooled cakes.

Grilled Meat Skewers

Ingredients: 1 lb (450g) meat (beef, lamb, or chicken), cut into cubes 1 zucchini, sliced 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks Cherry tomatoes, halved Salt and pepper Olive oil Skewers Instructions: Prepare Meat: Season meat cubes with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Skewer: Thread meat, zucchini, bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes onto skewers. Grill: Preheat grill to medium-high. Grill skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until meat is cooked through. Serve: Serve with additional grilled veggies or a side salad.

Spicy Stir-Fried Potatoes

Ingredients: 3 large potatoes, cut into matchsticks 2 tbsp vegetable oil 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste) or your preferred chili sauce 1 tbsp rice vinegar 1 tbsp sugar 1 tsp sesame oil Sesame seeds, for garnish Green onions, chopped, for garnish Instructions: Prep Potatoes: Soak potato matchsticks in water for 10 minutes. Drain and pat dry. Stir-Fry: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add potatoes and stir-fry until they start to soften. Sauce: Mix soy sauce, gochujang, rice vinegar, sugar, and sesame oil in a bowl. Pour over potatoes and stir well. Cook: Continue to cook until potatoes are fully cooked and coated with sauce. Garnish: Sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped green onions. Enjoy your spicy stir-fried potatoes!
